What services does Intexture Architecture offer?
We provide end-to-end interior design solutions including space planning, 3D visualization, furniture design, material selection, renovation consulting, and turnkey project execution for both residential and commercial spaces.
How does the interior design process work at Intexture?
Our process begins with understanding your needs and site visit, followed by concept design, layout planning, 3D renders, material finalization, and supervised execution. We keep you involved and informed at every stage.
Do you take up only residential projects or commercial ones too?
We specialize in both. From apartments, villas, and penthouses to retail spaces, cafes, and corporate offices — our team adapts the design to suit your space, audience, and brand story.
Can I hire Intexture only for design, without execution?
Yes, we offer flexible engagement models. You can opt for design-only services, design + execution, or even modular consultations depending on your project scope and needs.
How long does a typical interior design project take?
Timelines vary based on scale and complexity. On average, residential interiors take 8–12 weeks from design finalization to execution, while commercial projects may vary depending on size and compliance.
What cities do you currently operate in?
We are headquartered in [Insert City] and offer design services across India. For full-service execution, we currently operate in major metros like Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Pune, and Ahmedabad.
Do you work within specific budgets?
Absolutely. Budget transparency is key to our process. We tailor materials, furniture, and finishes to align with your budget—without compromising on design quality or functionality.
Why should I choose Intexture over other firms?
With over 25 years of combined design experience, a reputation for precision, and an eye for detail, Intexture blends innovation with practical execution. We don’t just create spaces—we craft personalized design experiences.
