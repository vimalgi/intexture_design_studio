About
Us.

At Intexture Design Studios, we believe that every space holds the potential to become a masterpiece. As a multidisciplinary team of interior designers, architects, and exterior space planners, we specialize in crafting environments that merge aesthetics with utility. From minimalist apartments to luxurious villas and innovative commercial hubs, we bring your ideas to life with thoughtful design and meticulous execution. Our creative process is client-centric, ensuring that every detail reflects your personality, lifestyle, and goals. With a passion for textures, materials, and harmony, we turn ordinary spaces into extraordinary experiences.

Our services go beyond just visual appeal we design for functionality, sustainability, and long-term value. Whether its transforming interiors, elevating curb appeal, or designing open-air landscapes, we approach each project with a blend of artistic insight and architectural precision. At Intexture Design Studios, collaboration is key we work hand-in-hand with clients, contractors, and vendors to deliver seamless results on time and within budget. With a growing portfolio across residential, commercial, and hospitality spaces, were setting new benchmarks in design excellence across India. Lets shape the future of your space, together.

Our clients

We proudly serve a diverse clientele including homeowners, real estate developers, corporate offices, cafes, retail brands, architects, and hospitality leaders. Whether it's a compact urban home or a sprawling commercial facility, we collaborate closely to bring each clients vision to life beautifully and efficiently.

Our mission

To create transformative interior and exterior spaces through thoughtful design, innovation, and collaboration. We are committed to delivering personalized solutions that elevate everyday living while embracing sustainability, creativity, and craftsmanship.

Our vision

To redefine the way people experience spaces by blending architectural brilliance with soulful design that inspires, functions, and endures. We envision a world where every home, office, and exterior speaks of elegance, comfort, and character.

We want to create comfortable spaces, where people can work, study, connect or come together.
